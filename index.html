What is Green Gene?

Green Gene is a way for you to judge the foods you eat not only by their nutritional value but by their sourcing. Our mission is to not only help you make healthy choice, but to also make choices that will benefit local farmers as well.
We believe it's important for you to know where your food comes from so you can make better informed decisions on what foods to buy.

Made with love by CSU Stan CS Club.
